01How much does a home renovation cost in Belfast?

Costs vary with scope, but a kitchen renovation in Belfast typically starts around £12,000, a bathroom renovation from £6,500, and a single-storey extension from £45,000. We provide a fixed, itemised quote after a free on-site consultation, so there are no surprises once work begins.

02How long does a house extension take?

Most single-storey extensions in Belfast take 10–14 weeks from breaking ground to handover, including planning and Building Control sign-off. Loft and garage conversions typically take 4–6 weeks.

03Do I need planning permission for a renovation in Belfast?

Many internal renovations, kitchen fit-outs and bathroom upgrades don't require planning permission. Extensions, loft conversions and exterior changes often do, or may fall under permitted development. We handle all planning applications and Building Control submissions to Belfast City Council on your behalf.

04Is Laganside Renovations licensed and insured?

Yes. We are fully licensed, carry public liability insurance, and every project is backed by a 10-year workmanship guarantee. Our tradespeople are vetted, NVQ-qualified, and directly employed rather than subcontracted where possible.

05Which areas of Belfast and Northern Ireland do you cover?

We renovate homes across Belfast — including South Belfast, East Belfast, the Cathedral Quarter and Stranmillis — as well as Holywood, Lisburn, Bangor, Newtownabbey and Carrickfergus.
